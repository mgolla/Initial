SAFETY FOCUS Rating: 3
Conducts a thorough Safety & Security Search - Conducts a thorough Safety check of equipment and fixtures(Doors,seats etc) in their emergency area. Conducts a detailed and thorough Security Search in their emergency area in accordance with guidelines issued by the Security department.
Security Cabin / Galley for take-off and Landing - Ensures that the cabin / Galley area are secured for take-off and landing. Passes the Cabin Secure Check on time, is seated and strapped in their assigned crew seat in time.
Door Operations - Adheres to SOP's related to Arming/Disarming and Opening/Closing of the assigned Cabin Exit, if assigned a Door, if not,is aware of associated SOP's.
Knowledge on Standard Operating Procedures - Proficient in handling Unruly,smoking customers, INAD/DEPU/DEPA,UMs/YP.
In flight Fire Prevention & Knowledge of Fire Fighting Procedures - Ovens are checked prior to start up, Gallery manned all the times, Patrolling of the cabin. Lavatories monitored at regular intervals. Is aware of procedures when dealing with in Flight Fire situations.
Post Landing Safety Duties Conducts a thorough security check, hand over item found. Infant seat belt and other equipment returned to the correct stowage. Ensure that all units/carts locked and sealed.
Knowledge of Decompression / Turbulance Procedures - Is proficient in Decompression procedures. Is able to identify the various intensities of turbulance and is familiar with the associated crew actions.
Knowledge of Emergency Procedures - Is proficient in Emergency procedures on ground and in flight, this identification and selection of ABP's, ABP briefing, brace positions, post impact duties and crowd control.
Knowledge of Aircraft Doors & Systems- Is knowledgable of various aircraft systems such as evacuation signal system, emergency lighting, communications, water & waste(water shut off valves)etc. Is aware of the minimum crew complement, door features & procedures, ground and ditching evacuation procedures.
Knowledge of First Aid Procesures - Is proficient in First Aid procedures associated with CPR and in dealing with minor and major in Flight Emergencies.
